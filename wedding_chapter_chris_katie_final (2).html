📌 Open Items Tracker

Use this section to surface your most pressing decisions. The items below are common wedding open items — edit them to match your actual situation. Critical items are things that will block progress or cost you money if delayed.

Critical · Venue
Book Venue
Top-tier venues in popular seasons book 12–18 months out. If not booked, this is your #1 priority.
Critical · Payments
Track Next Deposit Due
Add your next vendor payment deadline here so it doesn't slip.
Critical · Admin
Send Save-the-Dates
Should go out 9–12 months before the wedding. Popular destination or holiday weekends need even more lead time.
Critical · Hotels
Book Guest Hotel Blocks
Reach out to nearby hotels to hold a room block. Do this early — rates and availability change fast.
High · Music
Choose First Dance Song
You'll need this before your first dance lesson and before the DJ finalizes their set. Don't leave it to the last month.
High · Ceremony
Write Vows
Start early — aim for a draft at least 2 months out. Share with officiant at least 3–4 weeks before.
High · Legal
Get Marriage License
Most states require applying 30–90 days before the wedding. Check your state's specific rules and out-of-state requirements.
High · Food
Choose Wedding Cake & Baker
Popular bakers book months in advance. Schedule a tasting, decide on design, and sign a contract.
Medium · Music
Finalize All Song Choices
First dance, father-daughter, mother-son, processional, recessional, cocktail vibe, must-plays, do-not-plays — all needed by your DJ/band deadline.
Medium · Stationery
Order Invitations
Mail 8–10 weeks before the wedding. Allow 4–6 weeks for design + print. Order by ~4.5 months out.
Medium · Attire
Finalize All Attire & Fittings
Dress, suits, bridesmaids — coordinate fittings and pickup timelines with your planner.
Medium · Gifts
Wedding Party Gifts
Plan early if you want personalization — allow 4–6 weeks for custom items.
